Professional Journey

It is extremely difficult to succinctly describe my journey of 730+ days in words less than one-tenth of it. But lemme try. I have been working in a metaverse startup (WoWExp Technologies) for the last 2 years as a UX Designer.

Being the UX team of one, I was responsible for everything, the stakeholder interviews to developer handoff. In 2 years, my skills were honed horizontally as well as vertically.

Apart from this, I also have experience in designing websites for the E-commerce industry, B2B SaaS products for Social Media companies and Overseas Education Consultancy.
