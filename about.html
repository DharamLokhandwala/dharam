My principles

Communication & Collaboration

As designers, our job goes beyond ideating, sketching & prototyping; it's about collaborating and clearly communicating the ideas to stakeholders and cross-functional teams.

Presentation & Storytelling.

Effective presentation of ideas, concepts, and products to stakeholders, audiences, and teams is crucial, and storytelling is the key to this success. Inspired by Steve Jobs and David JP Phillips, I am dedicated to constantly refining my storytelling techniques to communicate my ideas in the most engaging and compelling way possible.

Evidence driven

Designs have an impact on people's lives. I believe in backing my design decisions with evidence because assumptions without data backing is just making a guess.

Ecosystem view

In design, I believe in a system-wide perspective, recognizing how changes in one area impact the entire experience ecosystem. This strategic approach ensures consistent user experiences across all touchpoints and identifies growth opportunities for the business.

Ethical

At my core, I believe in privacy of data and refuse to support dark patterns in design.

Inclusive

I ensure that every design speaks to a diverse range of needs and experiences, thus fostering a world where everyone feels valued and represented.
