Empathizing with client

Madhi ni Khamni is a very famous brand in our city and people love their food products. There is no person in my city who wouldn't have tasted their famous "Kamni", and I'm not exaggerating here!

Mr Hari, the owner of the company wanted to do something big for their company, which was never ever done by their ancestors in this field of business. He wanted to export his most famous product outside India, i.e, he wanted to sell Instant-Khamni to USA where there was a very good market for instant food items. So he wanted to show people that his company was established and trust-worthy.

Business Goals

1) To take their offline business online

2) To spread brand awareness and build Trust for new customers.

3) Showcase the newly started product “Ready to eat Khamni” and increase it’s product value for export
