Overview

Deep Sleep Sounds is an app designed to enhance your sleep quality. It offers a rich library of calming sounds and white noises, tailored to combat insomnia and stress. The app features a sleep timer and the ability to create custom soundscapes. With options ranging from relaxing music to nature sounds, it's an ideal solution for those seeking a restful night's sleep.

What I did(TLDR)

  • Researched for the relation between sleep and music.
  • Used personalization and prompts (To improve retention)
  • Altered the structure of "Sound player" (To improve engagement)
  • Added system feedbacks (To improve usability)
  • Improved onboarding process for more personalized feed
  • Re-structured the home screen (To improve clarity)
  • Improved the premium features visibility (To meet business goals)

Impact of redesign

Client's confusion

Why are our daily active users decreasing? Why are our downloads not increasing?

Challenge

To identify what really is the "problem" leading to drop of active users. And further, addressing it to increase retention and engagement metrics.

Is User Interface a problem?

Conducted Heuristic Evaluation to identify if the interface was an issue.

Key issues discovered:

  • Lack of system feedbacks.
  • Low visibility of system status.
  • Low match between system & real world.
  • Low user control.
  • Less error prevention.

Is the type of music recommended a problem?
or
Do people need their own preferred music?

Finding co-relation between sleep & music (specially preferred vs selected)

Insights & confirmation

It was clear that music did improve sleep and there was no difference in sleep quality between preferred music and our recommended music.

Why other apps are doing well?

Identifying patterns, ideas and inspiration from trending apps in this domain.

Findings from competitive analysis:

  • Better daily engagements through reminders and streaks
  • Short onboarding focusing on goals of the app
  • Clean and minimal homepage keeping in mind the frequent users
  • Habit forming through streaks
  • Avoid ending sleep sound sessions abruptly, but rather fade it

  • Selecting minutes is easier than setting end-time like an alarm.
  • Prefer to see countdown after setting sleep session time
