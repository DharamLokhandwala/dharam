Context

It's always been essential for me to grasp the psychology of my clients. This allows me to empathize with their objectives and understand the mental framework that drives their product development process.

About Exceptional App Studios

At Exceptional App Studios, their primary focus is on developing cutting-edge apps that empower individuals to achieve personal growth and development. Their unwavering vision to help people reach their full potential has resulted in the successful creation of apps that have served over 1,000,000 users to date.

About Deep Sound

Deep Sleep Sounds is a sleep music app that offers a variety of different high-quality relaxing melodies and sleeping sounds intended to help people who suffer from insomnia or have trouble falling asleep.

Challenge

To identify problems and possible changes which would lead to improving overall experience and increasing retention and engagement metrics.

What I did

  • Improved retention of users using personalization and prompts
  • Improved the engagement and structure of "Sound player" for better usability and system feedbacks
  • Redesigned the onboarding process for more personalized feed
  • Improved the usability and rethought the structure of home screen
  • Improved the premium features visibility

Exploration

Heuristic Review

Instead of directly diving into the visuals, I conducted a heuristic evaluation to identify strength and weakness from an interaction and usability point of view.

Main issues:

  • Lack of system feedbacks and status
  • Not enough flexible around personalized content
  • Lack of clarity in onboarding

Impact

20% increase in adoption rate
32% increase in session streaks
47% increase in Daily active users

The aforementioned data was gathered 60 days post the launch of the revamped app. The figures indicate a rise in sleep sessions per user, implying that individuals were now using Deep Sleep more frequently. The elevated utilization of sleep sessions suggests that users were able to achieve better quality sleep with the aid of this application. This outcome instilled a sense of drive within me to continue making a positive impact in people's lives through design.
